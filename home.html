.01 Description of the interface

Problem to solve: Help users to washing their clothes efficient

The interface of washing machine is showing on the top of the machine. There are three main components. One is use to power on or off the machine, working as the main controller of the washing machine. There is another button that is used to start or end the washing program. It could also pause the program if there is accident happening. The largest spin button on the interface is used to change the mode of washing depending on users' needs.

Pure observations

First Interviewee: M

Background: An office lady with regular schedule

Observation: She used the washing machine in the early evening. When starting the machine, she tried twice because the machine did not response at the first time. Then, she utilized the spin button to switch to the “Energy Saving” mode. Besides, she took over 30 seconds to change the temperature and select the clothes’ material to apply specification for washing. After setting up the mode and temperature, she poured some laundry detergent based on her previous experience in the box on the left side of the screen. Finally, she pushed the start button to start the washing program.

Second Interviewee: Alvin

Background: A college student with busy coursework

Observation:He has a basket to store the dirty clothes. He grabbed his clothes from the basket to fulfill the washing machine. He powered up the machine and throwed an tides pod to the machine, and subsequently closed the door within 3 seconds. After the screen has lighted up, he used 1 second to set up the “Spins Only” mode by applying the spin button. Then, he pushed the start button with another second. The whole process took him less than 10 seconds.

Third Interviewee: Gemma

Background: A master student approaching to graduate

Observation: She washed her clothes in an afternoon. She walked to the washing machine slowly with enough clothes to fulfill half of the machine. She throwed her pre-prepared tides pod to the machine and closed the door. When she powered up the machine, it seemed that she had some difficulty to push the button. She held the power button for 3 seconds to wait for the lighting up of the screen. Then, she used the spin button to choose the “Normal” mode and pushed the start button subsequently.

Interview Questions and Summary

List of Interview Questions

  • How often do you wash your clothes?
  • Will you categorize your clothes before washing?
  • What mode will you choose to use?
  • How long is the washing time?
  • Would you think of any disadvantages of the screen?

Summary of Response

  • Based on the answer from two females, M and Gemma, the frequency of washing clothes and interacting with the interface depends on how often they work out, while the only male, Alvin, states that he tends to wash clothes once a week when running out of all available clothes or pants.
  • Since Gemma and Alvin only use one mode when they are interacting with the interface of washing machine, they think all other functions on the interface could be simplified. However, M, as a housewife, changes the mode frequently based on the material of her clothes.
  • Both Gemma and M would like to spend more than an hour to wait for the completion of the washing machine, while Alvin always choose the mode for only 15 minutes, reducing the time spending on washing.
  • Gemma reflects some difficulty when interacting with the washing machine, including the hardship when pushing the button and the misleading signs on the screen.

.03 Personas - Woman with work and family

Description of the user: This is a persona describing a typical woman with work and a family. She would like to wash the clothes when coming back home after a workday. She would be a detailed person with the fully exploration of the functions on the interface of the washing machine.

Interface problem: Since she would like to change different washing modes for different clothes, the problem would be the difficulty and unclarity on the interface explaining the modes.

Reasons behind the persona: The persona includes the thinking process and feelings after a typical workday. Also, the persona points out the details when the person is going to utilize the function on the interface. Thus, the persona would represent how a woman with work and family uses the washing machine.

.03 Personas - Student

Description of the user: This is a persona describing a student with busy coursework. This type of user tends to wash clothes at any time with convenience. Since the person would be rushed for his or her coursework, he or she would not be able to pay attention to the different functions on the interface.

Interface problem: The main problem would be the responsiveness of the button on the interface. He or she wants to complete the washing duty as soon as possible, so he or she would not like to be stuck on the interface for a long time.

Reasons behind the persona:The persona includes the thinking process and feelings when a person in a rushed situation. It provides the information about how hurry the person is, so it is suitable for describing a student doing the washing duty.
